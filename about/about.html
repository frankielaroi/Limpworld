ALL ABOUT LIMPWORLD ↓

VISION

The organization would strive to create a safe and inclusive space where young people from diverse backgrounds could come together to share their ideas, skills, and experiences, and collaborate on projects that address pressing social and environmental issues.

The organization would aim to provide opportunities for youth to develop leadership skills, gain knowledge and experience in areas such as social entrepreneurship, community service, environmental sustainability, and global citizenship.

NGO would be to create a world where young people are empowered to be agents of change and make a positive impact on their communities and the world at large.

MISSION

LIMPWORLD would be to empower young people by providing them with the tools, resources, and opportunities they need to become active and engaged members of their communities.

LIMPWORLD would be to foster a sense of social responsibility among young people by providing them with opportunities to engage in advocacy . This could involve working with government and non-governmental organizations to influence policy decisions.

Encouraging young people to become advocates and activists for issues they are passionate about, and providing them with the skills and resources they need to effect change.

GOAL

Educating young people on key social and environmental issues, organizing campaigns and protests, and partnering with policymakers and community leaders to influence policy decisions that affect young people and their communities.

Foster a sense of community and belonging among young people by providing them with opportunities to connect with others and engage in positive social and environmental initiatives.
